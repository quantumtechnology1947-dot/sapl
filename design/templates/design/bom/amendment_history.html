About Amendment Tracking

Purpose: Amendment records preserve the historical state of BOM items when they are modified after the design finalization date.

When Created: Automatically created when a BOM item is updated after the work order's design finalization date has passed.

ECN Workflow: Items with amendments typically require an Engineering Change Notice (ECN) to document the reasons for changes.

Amendment Number: Increments with each modification to track the version history of the BOM item.
